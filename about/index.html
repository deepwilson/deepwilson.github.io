About Me

My name is Oluwaleke Umar Yusuf and I'm a mechanical engineer. I'm a highly resourceful mechanical engineer with over three years of industry experience in construction and telecommunications. I'm from Nigeria, living in Egypt and currently studying Robotics, Control and Smart Systems at the American University In Cairo. You can find me on or on .
